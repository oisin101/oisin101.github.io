Hijack Games is a games studio based in Ireland. Our friendly staff and friends have gathered into one collaboration of a team to embrace and stride forward our love for games into a powerful career. We first founded Hijack all the way back in 2008, with nothing except a garage to work inside of. But this did not stop us nor did it demotivate us. We pushed for more, for better and with great support from our community, family & friends alike we created something bigger we could have never achieved without them. A dream made true in the best sense.
