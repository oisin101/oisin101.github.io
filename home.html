Hijack's Vision

Here at Hijack we expect only the greatest and latest out of games, events and more. We have dedicated much time and effort towards our passion towards games in our lifelong goal for a free, fun experience for all things games to be available for everyone, wherever, whenever. We are proud to have built a foundation of kindness and respect among our fellow staff and friends alike.

Latest Developments at Hijack Games

As of the past few years we have grown a lot, and are excited to announce that...! Hijack Games will be expanding further! After much trouble in planning permission Hijack will be receiving an extension to our current studio along with a new building entirely! We are extremely proud to have come this far and are incredibly grateful for the people that have done nothing but provide us the finest support we could ever ask for. With these developments, we will be able to hire more staff and speed up, along with ensure higher quality of our future game projects - on this topic we can say that our next game will revolve around a RPG, dungeon theme and will have many gameplay elements we have come up with ourselves which we believe will provide a fun experience!
